Heart of England Forest #treesfortrees campaign

Get your pencils, pens, crayons, cameras and smart phones out and start generating tree pictures to share with us because the Heart of England Forest needs your help to plant a forest in a day.

The Heart of England Forest - Plant a Forest in a Day #treesfortrees event

How It Works
You may submit as many different photos and pictures of trees as you wish. Pictures don’t have to be of a tree – we want images of whatever a tree means to you. For each image we receive we’ll plant a tree for you on the 2nd November 2015. If we get more than 10,000 entries? We’ll do our absolute best to plant a tree for every image received and accepted by 23.59 2nd November 2015 and you can watch the progress of our virtual forest at www.treesfortrees.co.uk.

One lucky participant will be selected at random to name the forested area!

How to Enter
  • Share your photos, pictures or images between Monday 26th October 2015 and 23.59 on Monday 2nd November 2015 on Twitter using @The_HOEF and #treesfortrees, on the #treesfortrees post of our Facebook page at facebook.com/heartofenglandforest or upload to Instagram using #treesfortrees or email to treesfortrees@hoef.co.uk
  • If you are using Instagram or Twitter, please adjust your privacy settings so that we can see your entry and contact you if you are the lucky winner.
  • For every image received which is deemed to be appropriately tree-related and is placed within our virtual forest on www.treesfortrees.co.uk a physical tree will be planted for you on 2nd November 2015 as we aim to plant a forest in a day.
